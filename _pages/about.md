Prof. Fredrik Lindsten](https://liu.se/medarbetare/freli29) and co-supervised by [Asst. Prof. Jens Sjölund](https://jsjol.github.io/) and [Asst. Prof. Zheng Zhao](https://zz.zabemon.com/).
